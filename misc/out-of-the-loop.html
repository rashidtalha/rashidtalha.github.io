Out of the Loop

This is a social deduction game where each person is given a secret word. One person is the imposter. They are given a different word (but from the same category). Everyone else gets the same word. Players then take turns asking questions to see who is acting suspicious. The goal is to catch the imposter by voting. If the imposter is caught, they get one last chance to win if they can figure out the word that the rest of the group was given.

Feel free to modify these rules, or add your own. For example you can limit the number of questions or the type of questions that can be asked before the voting begins.
